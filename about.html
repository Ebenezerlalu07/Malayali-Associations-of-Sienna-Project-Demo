MAS – Malayali Association of Sienna

Established in 2025 in Missouri City, Houston, the Malayali Association of Sienna (MAS) was formed to unite the local Malayali community and celebrate the rich cultural traditions of Kerala. MAS serves as a vibrant platform to preserve and promote Kerala’s heritage while nurturing a strong sense of identity and belonging among its members.

Through a wide range of cultural, social, and educational events, MAS creates opportunities for families and future generations to stay connected to their roots. The association is committed to fostering inclusivity, unity, and cultural pride within the Sienna and the greater Houston area.

In addition to its cultural mission, MAS actively participates in civic responsibilities, engaging in community service, public awareness programs, and collaborative efforts that contribute to the well-being of the wider society. Whether it's volunteering, supporting local causes, or promoting civic engagement, MAS encourages its members to be responsible, informed citizens.
